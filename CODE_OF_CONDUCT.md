# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Laravel ScratchPad to be a welcoming, respectful project for people with different backgrounds, experience levels, identities, and viewpoints.

Contributors and maintainers are expected to act with empathy, patience, and care in issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and any other project space.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different opinions and experiences.
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully.
- Keep discussions focused on what helps the project and its users.
- Assume good intent, while taking responsibility for your impact.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, stalking, or sustained disruption.
- Insults, demeaning comments, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably make someone feel unsafe or unwelcome.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, reject contributions, or temporarily or permanently ban participants whose behavior violates this code of conduct.

If you need to report an incident, email the maintainer listed in `package.json`. Reports will be handled as privately as practical, with care for the safety of the people involved.
